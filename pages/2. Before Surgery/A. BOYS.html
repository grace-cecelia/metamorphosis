Build Out Your Surgery (BOYS) Tool

This tool can show you what meta might look like on your body. You can choose from different types of meta, different skin tones, and different body types to see what your post-surgery body may look like.

While anyone can explore this tool, we recommend that you know a little bit about meta before using it. If you don’t already have some background knowledge, you might find it helpful to read the Overview first.
